LMM-10K
Large-Scale 4K Multimodal Dataset

Overview

LMM-10K is a large-scale multimodal video dataset consisting of 10,000 high-quality 4K videos at 60 fps enriched with semantic, perceptual, and content-aware annotations.

  • 📹 10,000 sampled 4K video sequences from Pexels
  • 🧠 Rich multimodal annotations combining semantic and perceptual information
  • 🤖 LLM-generated semantic descriptors using the Qwen3 model
  • 🎯 No-reference quality assessment using PIQE, BRISQUE, NIMA, and LIQE
  • 📊 Video complexity analysis including SI/TI, VCA , and EVCA
  • 🌍 Broad diversity of real-world scenes covering natural, urban, and dynamic content
  • ⚙️ Designed to support a wide range of applications:
    • Video enhancement and restoration
    • Content-aware video compression
    • Neural video coding
    • Multimodal learning (vision-language)
    • Generative video modeling
    • Perceptual quality assessment
