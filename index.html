LMM-10K Dataset

A Large-Scale Multimodal Video Dataset for Visual, Perceptual, and Semantic Understanding

Overview

LMM-10K is a large-scale dataset consisting of 10,000 high-quality 4K videos at 60fps, enriched with multimodal annotations including LLM-generated captions, quality metrics, and spatio-temporal complexity features.

Teaser

Key Features

  • 10,000 high-fidelity 4K videos (60fps)
  • LLM-generated semantic captions
  • No-reference quality metrics
  • Spatial-temporal complexity annotations
  • Diverse real-world content
