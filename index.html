LMM-10K
Large-Scale 4K Multimodal Dataset

Overview

LMM-10K is a large-scale multimodal video dataset consisting of 10,000 high-quality 4K videos at 60 fps enriched with semantic, perceptual, and content-aware annotations.

  • 📹 10,000 curated 4K video sequences
  • 🧠 Multimodal annotations (semantic + perceptual)
  • 📊 Video complexity & quality metrics
  • 🌍 Diverse real-world scenes from Pexels
  • ⚙️ Designed for AI, compression, and multimodal research

Applications

  • Video enhancement and restoration
  • Content-aware video compression
  • Neural video coding
  • Multimodal learning (vision-language)
  • Generative video modeling
  • Perceptual quality modeling
